Hello! I'm Pavithra Chandrasekhar.

I'm UX researcher & designer who codes. I'm from Chennai, India living in New York City, United States. I recently completed my Masters in Design & Technology at Parsons. I'm passionate about public interest technology, open data, and digital privacy & security. I pursue work at the intersection of technology, design & society.

I'm interested in designing products and experiences that are easy to use, secure, and accesible. Prior to my time in Parsons, I worked at 22 Feet Tribal Worldwide as a Designer developing brand communication & campaigns. My experience in digital marketing has helped me approach design strategically. My work from my time there has been recognized by Kyoorius, Dotcom Awards and Social Samosa.

Holding a bachelors in Visual Communication from M.O.P. Vaishnav College for Women my practice is informed by Communication Design and I build experiences through impactful designs that simplifies & beautifies the user journey.

If you want to talk about any of that, I'm always down to chat.
